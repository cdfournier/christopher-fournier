Case StudyTransferX

TransferX is a blockchain-driven P2P money transfer platform.

Scroll

TransferX Project Details

Deliverables

Product design, logo design, branding, and UX design

Brief

Design a premium user experience and brand identity for a blockchain financial services startup based in the UK.

Audience

Individuals who want a secure and easy way to transfer money to friends and family, especially in unbanked parts of the world.

Strategy

Design intuitively modern and sophisticated native app screens for a simple user experience that feels as familiar as fiat payment apps.

Intended Outcomes

Deliver a minimal viable product to test with early customers in eastern African nations like Kenya, Ethiopia, and Tanzania and advise on future product development.

Details

Fintech apps focused on helping facilitate P2P crypto and fiat transfers, especially in developing and largely unbanked regions, are one of the most viable uses for blockchain. To foster the adoption of crypto payments, the app UX must feel as seamless to the user as using popular conventional payment apps.

My Process

To get started on the branding I first created a mood board with images of people, places, and things in locations such as Kenya, Ethiopia, and Tanzania. The color palette emerged with vibrant reds, yellows, greens, and blues. Next I designed some patterns inspired by the arts and traditions of various African communities in order to provide some subtle texture. The founders had stated that they did not want the brand to feel “sterile”.

When it came to the logo, I started by designing the icon. I wanted the icon to express confidence and stability. The X in TransferX became an arrow to signify transactions and progress. I was keen to have something that would look good in any form of branding, as well as stand out from a crowd of blockchain logos and icons.

In order to better understand the opportunities and challenges of the app design and user experience, I conducted a series of interviews with people involved in the blockchain space. Based on these insights, I designed a user experience with a focus on:

  1. A modern, mobile-first responsive design
  2. Simplified navigation for native and non-native crypto users
  3. Complex options for payments, including repeat and automated payment scheduling abilities

Outcome

The end result is a beautiful, modern, and intuitive app that makes transferring funds easy and enjoyable.
